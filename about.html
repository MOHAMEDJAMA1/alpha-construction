Our Story

Founded in 1995, Alpha Construction began with a simple mission: to build with integrity and quality. Over the last three decades, we have grown from a small local contractor into a premier construction firm, handling major projects across the region.

Our success is built on a foundation of trust. We believe in transparency with our clients, safety for our workers, and sustainability for the environment.

To deliver exceptional construction services that exceed expectations, leveraging cutting-edge technology and time-honored craftsmanship.
